About Pinnacle Group

Since its inception, the Pinnacle Group has always put people first. We pride ourselves on our understanding of the needs of our customers and revel in the value they derive out of our creations. We believe in long term associations and nurture warm bonds

Our dedicated and professional team gives meticulous attention to every project, striving always at the twin goals of precision and perfection. Innovation is at the core of everything we do. We insist on being exclusive. Our homes are loved by those who reside in them. That’s what delights us the most.

For us, Construction is a passion, not a profession. All our projects are built on a solid foundation of insight and thoughtfulness. Our relentless pursuit of perfection has made us one of the most respected players in the Pune real estate market. The innovation and exclusivity that we bring to each project translate into the pride of ownership – making our customers feel like a privileged few.

We, at the Pinnacle Group, believe that we are in the business of delighting our customers. Allow us once again to delight you.
